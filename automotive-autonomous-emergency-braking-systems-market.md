<p><p>Automotive Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) systems enhance vehicle safety by automatically applying brakes to prevent collisions. The market is segmented into Two-Box and One-Box systems. Two-Box systems separate the radar/lidar sensor and control unit, offering flexibility and scalability for diverse vehicle platforms. One-Box systems integrate the sensor and control unit into a single module, reducing size, cost, and complexity, ideal for compact vehicles. Both types aim to improve safety, with Two-Box focusing on adaptability and One-Box emphasizing compactness and affordability.</p></p>
