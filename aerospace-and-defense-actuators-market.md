<p><p>The Aerospace and Defense Actuators Market is divided into two primary types: rotary actuators and linear actuators. Rotary actuators control the angular movement of components, converting energy into rotational motion, essential for applications like flap and valve operations in aircraft. Linear actuators, on the other hand, provide linear motion through mechanical means, crucial for tasks such as landing gear deployment and surface control movements. Both actuator types are vital for enhancing performance and reliability in aerospace and defense systems.</p></p>
