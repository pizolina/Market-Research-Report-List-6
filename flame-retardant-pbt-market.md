<p><p>The Flame Retardant PBT market is divided into two primary types: halogen flame retardants and halogen-free flame retardants. Halogen flame retardants incorporate chlorine or bromine compounds, providing effective resistance to combustion but raising environmental and health concerns. In contrast, halogen-free flame retardants utilize alternative chemical formulations to enhance fire safety without the associated risks of halogenated compounds. The choice between these types primarily depends on regulatory requirements, application-specific performance, and environmental considerations in various industries.</p></p>
