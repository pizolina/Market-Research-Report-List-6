<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) Motor market comprises different types, including Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS) motors, Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS) motors, Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS) motors, and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) motors. CEPS motors are integrated into the steering column for compact designs. REPS motors are attached to the steering rack, providing direct assistance. PEPS motors combine electrical and mechanical components, while EHPS motors use hydraulic assistance, blending electric and hydraulic systems for enhanced steering performance.</p></p>
