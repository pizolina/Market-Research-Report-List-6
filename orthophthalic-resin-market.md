<p><p>Orthophthalic resin is categorized into two main types: filled resin and unfilled resin. Filled resin incorporates additives such as glass fibers or minerals, enhancing properties like strength and thermal stability, making it suitable for applications in construction and automotive industries. Unfilled resin, on the other hand, is pure and offers excellent clarity and chemical resistance, ideal for applications like coatings and molded parts. Each type serves specific industry needs, influencing their market demand and application scope.</p></p>
