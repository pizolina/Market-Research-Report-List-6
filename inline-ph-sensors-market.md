<p><p>Inline pH sensors are essential tools for monitoring pH levels in various industrial applications. Combination pH sensors integrate both the glass electrode and the reference electrode into a single unit, providing accurate and stable measurements. Differential pH sensors, on the other hand, measure the pH by comparing two different points in a solution, enhancing precision in fluctuating conditions. Other types may include specialized sensors for extreme environments or specific applications, catering to diverse industrial needs and ensuring optimal process control.</p></p>
