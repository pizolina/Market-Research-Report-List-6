<p><ul><li>Front Systems</li><li>Surround System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio distribution systems can be categorized into front systems and surround systems. Front systems primarily focus on delivering sound from the front, often utilizing two or more speaker setups to create stereo sound, ideal for music and announcements. In contrast, surround systems immerse listeners in sound by utilizing multiple speakers positioned around a room, enhancing the audio experience for movies and gaming. Each market type targets different consumer needs, emphasizing either clarity and direct sound or a more immersive audio environment.</p></p>
