<p><p>A photoresist spinner is a specialized piece of equipment used in the semiconductor manufacturing process to apply a uniform layer of photoresist onto a substrate, typically silicon wafers. This process is crucial for photolithography, where patterns are etched onto the substrate to create integrated circuits and electronic devices. The spinner usually operates by spinning the wafer at high speeds, allowing the photoresist to spread evenly due to centrifugal force.</p><p>The Photoresist Spinner Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.50% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for miniaturization of electronic components and advancements in semiconductor technologies.
