<p><p>A System on Module (SoM) is a compact, integrated circuit that integrates most components of a complete system, including a microprocessor, memory, and input/output interfaces, onto a single board. SoMs are designed to streamline the development of embedded systems, allowing developers to focus on application-specific features without the need to design the entire hardware stack from scratch. They are widely used in various sectors, including industrial automation, automotive, telecommunications, and the Internet of Things (IoT).</p><p>The System on Module market is poised for significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for miniaturization in electronic devices and the need for faster product development cycles.
