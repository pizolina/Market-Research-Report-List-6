<p><ul><li>Softeners</li><li>Reverse Osmosis</li><li>Ion Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-Scale Systems market comprises three primary types: water Softeners, Reverse Osmosis (RO), and Ion Exchange systems. Softeners primarily reduce hardness in water by removing calcium and magnesium ions, preventing scale buildup in pipes and appliances. Reverse Osmosis employs a semipermeable membrane to filter out impurities, including scale-forming minerals. Ion Exchange systems exchange undesirable ions in water with more benign ones, effectively reducing scale and improving water quality. Together, these technologies enhance water treatment and protect infrastructure.</p></p>
