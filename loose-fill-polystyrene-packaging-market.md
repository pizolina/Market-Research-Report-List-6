<p><p>The loose fill polystyrene packaging market comprises various types, including ordinary polystyrene, expanded polystyrene (EPS), high-impact polystyrene (HIPS), and specialty polystyrene (SPS). Ordinary polystyrene is a rigid plastic, primarily used in general applications. EPS is lightweight and offers excellent cushioning, making it ideal for protecting fragile items during shipping. HIPS combines strength with impact resistance, suitable for food packaging. SPS is engineered for specific applications, providing tailored properties for unique packaging needs, enhancing performance across diverse industries.</p></p>
