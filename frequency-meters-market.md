<p><strong>The Frequency Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deflection Type</li><li>Resonant Reed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frequency meters market includes two primary types: deflection type and resonant reed type. Deflection type frequency meters measure electrical frequency by displaying the deflection of a needle on a scale, providing a direct visual reading, typically used in laboratory settings. Resonant reed type frequency meters utilize vibrating reeds that resonate at specific frequencies, offering high precision for low-frequency measurements. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, including telecommunications, electronics testing, and industrial monitoring, ensuring accurate frequency analysis.</p></p>
