<p><strong>The Automotive Seat Belt Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>anchor and buckle pretensioners</li><li>seat belt retractor pretensioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat belt pretensioner market is categorized into three main types: anchor pretensioners, buckle pretensioners, and seat belt retractor pretensioners. Anchor pretensioners work by tightening the belt at the anchor point during a collision, minimizing slack. Buckle pretensioners are integrated into the buckle assembly, pulling the belt snug upon impact. Seat belt retractor pretensioners utilize a retraction mechanism to reduce belt slack instantly. Each type enhances occupant safety by improving the effectiveness of seat belts during accidents.</p></p>
