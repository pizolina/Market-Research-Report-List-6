<p><p>The lung cancer molecular diagnostics market encompasses various technologies to detect genetic mutations and alterations associated with the disease. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences for mutation identification. FISH (Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization) visualizes genetic changes at the chromosomal level. DNA sequencing provides detailed insights into the genetic makeup of tumors. Gene chips, or microarrays, analyze multiple genes simultaneously to identify expression patterns. Together, these techniques enhance personalized treatment approaches and improve patient outcomes in lung cancer management.</p></p>
