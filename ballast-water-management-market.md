<p><p>The Ballast Water Management Market encompasses three main types: Physical Disinfection, Mechanical Methods, and Chemical Methods. </p><p>Physical Disinfection includes techniques such as UV radiation and thermal treatment to eradicate aquatic organisms. Mechanical Methods involve filtration systems that physically remove unwanted particles from ballast water. Chemical Methods utilize biocides to kill invasive species, often involving substances that can pose environmental concerns. Each method aims to reduce the introduction of non-native species into marine environments, ensuring compliance with international regulations.</p></p>
