Medium strength options strike a balance, providing adequate grip for moderate loads while still allowing for eventual disassembly. High strength retaining compounds are designed for heavy-duty applications, offering maximum bond strength to withstand extreme pressures and temperatures, often used in critical machinery and automotive components where durability is paramount.</p></p>
