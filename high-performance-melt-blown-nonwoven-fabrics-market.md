<p><p>The high-performance melt-blown nonwoven fabrics market comprises primarily two types: single-layer and double-layer. Single-layer nonwovens consist of a singular, uniform layer offering basic filtration and barrier properties, suitable for applications like masks and basic protective gear. In contrast, double-layer nonwovens feature two distinct layers, enhancing filtration efficiency and structural integrity, making them ideal for higher-demand uses such as advanced medical applications and durable consumer products. Both types cater to various industries, driven by performance and application needs.</p></p>
