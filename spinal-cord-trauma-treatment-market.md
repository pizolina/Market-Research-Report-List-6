<p><p>The spinal cord trauma treatment market is primarily categorized into complete and partial spinal cord injuries. Complete spinal cord injuries result in total loss of motor and sensory function below the injury site, necessitating comprehensive care and rehabilitation strategies, often involving surgical intervention and long-term management. In contrast, partial spinal cord injuries allow for some preservation of function and sensation, leading to targeted therapies that focus on recovery and rehabilitation. Both categories require specialized treatment approaches to optimize patient outcomes.</p></p>
