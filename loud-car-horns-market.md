Air horns use compressed air to produce a powerful sound, often favored for their loudness and simple installation. Electronic horns generate sound electronically and are known for their variety of tones and ease of use. Electromagnetic horns employ an electromagnet to create sound, delivering a more traditional horn sound. Each type serves different preferences and applications in automotive signaling and safety.</p></p>
