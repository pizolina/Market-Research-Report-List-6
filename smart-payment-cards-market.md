<p><strong>The Smart Payment Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-based Smart Payment Cards</li><li>Contactless Smart Payment Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Payment Cards market is divided into two primary types: Contact-based and Contactless Smart Payment Cards. Contact-based Smart Payment Cards require physical insertion into a payment terminal, enabling secure transactions through direct contact. In contrast, Contactless Smart Payment Cards utilize RFID or NFC technology, allowing users to make payments by simply tapping their card near a compatible terminal. The contactless option offers faster, more convenient transactions, while contact-based cards provide an extra layer of security through direct interaction.</p></p>
