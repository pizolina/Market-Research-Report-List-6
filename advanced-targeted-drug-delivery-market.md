<p><p>The Advanced Targeted Drug Delivery Market encompasses several innovative types designed for enhancing therapeutic efficacy. Liposomes are lipid-based vehicles that encapsulate drugs, improving bioavailability and reducing side effects. Microspheres, small spherical particles, enable controlled release and targeted distribution of therapeutics. Nanoparticles, engineered at the nanoscale, enhance drug solubility and cellular uptake, facilitating precise delivery to diseased tissues. Emulsions, mixtures of oil and water, improve the stability and absorption of hydrophobic drugs, further optimizing their therapeutic impact.</p></p>
