<p><strong>The Automotive Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Wire</li><li>Hot Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensors market primarily consists of two types: Hot Wire and Hot Film sensors. Hot Wire sensors utilize a heated wire element to measure air flow by detecting changes in temperature as air passes over it, offering high sensitivity and response time. Hot Film sensors, on the other hand, employ a thin film element for similar purposes but are less sensitive to dirt and deposits, making them more durable. Both types contribute significantly to optimizing engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
