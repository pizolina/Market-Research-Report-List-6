<p><p>Form in Place (FIP) gaskets are a specialized sealing solution that involves applying a liquid or paste sealant directly onto the surface of the substrate, which then cures to form a solid gasket. <p><p>The Form in Place (FIP) Gaskets market is segmented into two main types: conductive and non-conductive gaskets. Conductive FIP gaskets are designed to provide electrical conductivity, making them suitable for applications requiring EMI/RFI shielding or grounding. Non-conductive FIP gaskets, on the other hand, are used for sealing applications where electrical insulation is crucial, preventing short circuits and ensuring safety. Both types offer tailored solutions for various industries, enhancing performance and reliability in sealing applications.</p></p>
