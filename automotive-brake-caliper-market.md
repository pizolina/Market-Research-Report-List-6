<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive brake caliper market is divided into two main types: single piston and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize one piston to apply pressure to the brake pads, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, suitable for lower performance vehicles. In contrast, multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons that provide enhanced braking performance, heat dissipation, and improved pad contact, making them ideal for high-performance and heavy-duty vehicles. Each type caters to different automotive needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
