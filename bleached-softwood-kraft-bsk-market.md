<p><p>Bleached Softwood Kraft (BSK) is a high-quality pulp derived from softwood trees, known for its strength and brightness, making it suitable for producing premium paper products. The market is segmented into Southern Bleached Softwood Kraft (SBSK) and Northern Bleached Softwood Kraft (NBSK). SBSK, sourced from southern pine species, typically has a lower kappa number and higher brightness, ideal for printing papers. In contrast, NBSK, sourced from northern coniferous species, offers greater tensile strength, preferred for applications requiring durability.</p></p>
