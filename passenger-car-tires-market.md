<p><p>The passenger car tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature cords arranged at a right angle to the tread, providing improved traction, durability, and fuel efficiency, making them ideal for modern vehicles. Conversely, bias tires have cords that are layered diagonally, offering a stiffer sidewall and better performance in off-road conditions but typically less fuel efficiency and endurance. Each type caters to different driving preferences and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
