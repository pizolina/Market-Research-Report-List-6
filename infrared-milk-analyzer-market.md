<p><p>The infrared milk analyzer market is categorized primarily into Near-Infrared (NIR) and Mid-Infrared (MIR) segments. NIR analyzers utilize wavelengths between 750 to 2500 nm to assess milk composition, offering rapid results and non-destructive testing. They are often favored for routine quality control due to their speed. MIR analyzers operate in the range of 2500 to 25,000 nm, providing more detailed information on milk constituents but typically require more time for analysis. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring milk quality and safety.</p></p>
