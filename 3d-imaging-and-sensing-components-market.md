<p><strong>The 3D Imaging and Sensing Components Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sensing Technologies: Stereo</li><li>Sensing Technologies: Structured light</li><li>Sensing Technologies: ToF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D imaging and sensing components market comprises various sensing technologies. Stereo sensing uses two or more cameras to capture depth information by analyzing disparities in images. Structured light involves projecting a known pattern onto an object to determine its shape and depth by analyzing the deformation of the pattern. Time-of-Flight (ToF) technology measures the time taken for a light signal to bounce back from an object, enabling accurate distance measurements for real-time 3D imaging applications.</p></p>
