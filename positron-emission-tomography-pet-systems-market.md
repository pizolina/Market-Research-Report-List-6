<p><p>The Positron Emission Tomography (PET) systems market can be categorized into three types: direct imaging, indirect imaging, and alternative imaging. Direct imaging involves capturing images from the emitted positrons in real-time, providing high-resolution data. Indirect imaging uses a secondary method, such as a scintillation detector, to convert gamma rays into visible light for imaging. Alternative imaging encompasses novel techniques or combinations with other imaging modalities, enhancing diagnostic capabilities and providing innovative solutions for various medical applications.</p></p>
