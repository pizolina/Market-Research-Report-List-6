<p><p>The liquid roofing market comprises several key types. Polyurethane (PU) coatings offer excellent durability and weather resistance. Acrylic coatings are favored for their UV stability and ease of application. PU/Acrylic hybrids combine the strengths of both materials, providing enhanced performance. Bituminous roofing is known for its waterproofing properties, commonly used in commercial applications. Silicone coatings are flexible, resistant to UV rays, and ideal for protecting roofs from ponding water, contributing to effective long-term maintenance strategies.</p></p>
