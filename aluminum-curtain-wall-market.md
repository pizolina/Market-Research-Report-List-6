<p><p>The aluminum curtain wall market comprises two primary types: semi-unitized and unitized systems. Semi-unitized curtain walls are prefabricated in sections that are assembled on-site, allowing for flexibility in design and installation. In contrast, unitized systems are completely fabricated and delivered as large panels, providing faster installation and enhanced quality control. Both types serve to enhance building aesthetics and performance, offering energy efficiency and weather resistance, but differ in installation processes and structural complexity.</p></p>
