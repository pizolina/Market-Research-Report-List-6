<p><p>The dental steam generators market consists of two main types: integral boilers and remote boilers. Integral boilers are compact units integrated within dental chairs or equipment, providing direct steam for sterilization and procedures. They are ideal for limited space environments. Remote boilers, on the other hand, are standalone systems that supply steam to multiple dental units or rooms, offering greater capacity and efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring proper sterilization and operational efficiency in dental practices.</p></p>
