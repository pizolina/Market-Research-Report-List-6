<p><p>The in vitro organ preservation market includes three main types: Static Cold Storage, Hypothermic Machine Perfusion, and Normothermic Machine Perfusion. Static Cold Storage utilizes low temperatures to prolong organ viability during transport. Hypothermic Machine Perfusion actively circulates a preservation solution at reduced temperatures, enhancing nutrient delivery and waste removal. Normothermic Machine Perfusion maintains organs at body temperature, supporting metabolic functions and improving viability for transplantation. Each method addresses specific challenges in organ preservation to optimize transplant outcomes.</p></p>
