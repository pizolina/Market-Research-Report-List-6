<p><p>The flexible plywood market is divided into three primary segments based on thickness: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Plywood under 6mm is typically used for applications requiring lightweight and easily bendable materials, such as in the production of curved surfaces. The 6mm-18mm category features mid-range thickness, suitable for furniture and cabinetry, combining flexibility and structural integrity. Plywood above 18mm is primarily used in heavy-duty applications, providing strength and durability for construction and commercial uses, while still allowing some degree of flexibility.</p></p>
