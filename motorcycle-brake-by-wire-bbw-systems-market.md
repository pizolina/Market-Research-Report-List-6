<p><ul><li>Single-channel</li><li>Multiple-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motorcycle Brake By Wire (BBW) systems are categorized into single-channel and multiple-channel markets. Single-channel systems feature one brake control circuit, typically managing either the front or rear brake, offering a simpler design and cost-effective solution for basic models. In contrast, multiple-channel systems utilize separate circuits for both front and rear brakes, enhancing safety and control by enabling more precise braking responses. This advanced technology is favored in high-performance motorcycles, providing improved stability and responsiveness during various riding conditions.</p></p>
