<p><p>The Geotechnical & Structural Monitoring Instruments market encompasses various types of specialized devices used to assess ground and structural stability. Inclinometers measure the angle of slope or tilt in soil or structures, ensuring safety in construction. Extensometers gauge changes in distance between two points, useful for monitoring strain in materials. Piezometers assess pore water pressure within soils, critical for evaluating groundwater conditions. Other instruments may include load cells and tilt sensors, collectively enhancing understanding of geotechnical and structural integrity.</p></p>
