<p><p>The timber framing market is categorized into three main types: softwood, hardwood, and massive timber. Softwood, derived from coniferous trees, is commonly used for construction due to its lightweight and cost-effectiveness. Hardwood, sourced from deciduous trees, offers durability and aesthetic appeal, making it ideal for high-end interiors and furniture. Massive timber refers to large, solid wood elements, often used in sustainable building practices for their thermal efficiency and structural strength. Each type serves distinct architectural and design needs.</p></p>
