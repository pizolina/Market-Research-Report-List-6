<p><p>The Linear Queue Management System encompasses various market types to streamline service processes. Structured Queues feature organized lines where customers wait in a defined order, ensuring efficiency. Unstructured Queues allow free-form lines, often leading to chaos but providing flexibility. Kiosk-Based Queues utilize self-service kiosks for customers to check in, enhancing convenience and reducing wait times. Moving Queues involve dynamic, fluctuating lines that adapt based on real-time customer flow, optimizing service delivery and resource allocation in busy environments.</p></p>
