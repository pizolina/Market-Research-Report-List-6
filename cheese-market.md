<p><p>The cheese market is categorized into four main types based on texture and aging. Soft cheese, like Brie, is creamy and often used in spreads. Semi-soft cheese, such as Gouda, is pliable and melty, making it popular for cooking. Medium-hard cheese, like Cheddar, has a firmer texture with a rich flavor, ideal for snacking and cooking. Hard cheese, such as Parmesan, is aged longer, has a dense texture, and is typically grated, enhancing various dishes with its strong flavor.</p></p>
