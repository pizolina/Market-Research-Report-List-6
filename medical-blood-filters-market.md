<p><ul><li>Whole Blood Transfusion</li><li>Platelet Transfusion</li><li>Red Cell Transfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical blood filters market is categorized into three primary types: whole blood transfusion, platelet transfusion, and red cell transfusion. Whole blood transfusion involves filtering whole blood to remove contaminants and ensure safety for patients receiving large volumes of blood. Platelet transfusion focuses on filtering platelet-rich plasma to enhance clotting capabilities while minimizing immune reactions. Red cell transfusion emphasizes the removal of leukocytes and other impurities from red blood cells to reduce transfusion-related complications and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
