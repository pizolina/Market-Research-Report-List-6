<p><strong>The Hydrogenated Palm Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Hydrogenated Palm Oil</li><li>Partially Hydrogenated Palm Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogenated palm oil market comprises two primary types: fully hydrogenated palm oil and partially hydrogenated palm oil. Fully hydrogenated palm oil undergoes complete hydrogenation, resulting in a stable, solid fat with high resistance to oxidation, making it ideal for baked goods and margarine. Conversely, partially hydrogenated palm oil retains some unsaturated fats, offering desired texture and shelf life but is linked to health concerns, leading to declining use. Both types serve distinct applications in food and industrial sectors.</p></p>
