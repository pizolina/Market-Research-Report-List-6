<p><p>The Broadband Anti-Reflective Coating market is segmented into two main types: Single Layer and Multilayer coatings. Single Layer coatings typically consist of one thin film designed to minimize reflection at specific wavelengths, making them effective for limited applications. In contrast, Multilayer coatings involve multiple layers of varying materials, which can be engineered to reduce reflection over a broader range of wavelengths. This versatility allows Multilayer coatings to enhance optical performance in diverse applications, from telecommunications to consumer electronics.</p></p>
