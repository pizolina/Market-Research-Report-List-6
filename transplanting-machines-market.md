<p><p>The transplanting machines market is categorized into three main types: riding type, walking type, and tractor-mounted. Riding type machines are designed for operators to sit and maneuver while transplanting crops efficiently over larger areas. Walking type machines require the operator to walk alongside, offering flexibility for smaller fields. Tractor-mounted machines attach to tractors, utilizing their power for high-capacity transplanting, making them ideal for large-scale farming operations. Each type serves distinct agricultural needs based on field size and crop types.</p></p>
