<p><strong>The Remote Keyless Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry Systems</li><li>Active Keyless Entry Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remote keyless systems market comprises two primary types: passive keyless entry systems and active keyless entry systems. </p><p>Passive keyless entry systems allow users to unlock and start their vehicles without needing to press any buttons, typically using proximity sensors that detect the key fob when the user approaches. In contrast, active keyless entry systems require users to press a button on their key fob to unlock or start their vehicle, providing a more traditional approach to vehicle access and security. Both systems enhance convenience and security for vehicle users.</p></p>
