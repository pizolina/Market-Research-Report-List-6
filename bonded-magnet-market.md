<p><strong>The Bonded Magnet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Injected Magnets</li><li>Plastic Pressed Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bonded magnet market is primarily categorized into two types: plastic injected magnets and plastic pressed magnets. Plastic injected magnets are produced by injecting a polymer matrix mixed with magnetic powders into a mold, allowing for complex shapes and high production rates. In contrast, plastic pressed magnets involve compacting a mixture of magnetic powders and polymer under heat and pressure, resulting in a denser product with enhanced magnetic properties. Both types cater to various applications, including electric motors, sensors, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
