<p><p>The medical syringe pump market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel syringe pumps. Single-channel syringe pumps are designed to deliver medication through one syringe, making them ideal for straightforward, individual patient needs. In contrast, multi-channel syringe pumps accommodate multiple syringes, allowing simultaneous administration of different medications or dosages to multiple patients or a single patient, enhancing efficiency in clinical settings. Both types offer precision in drug delivery, catering to varying healthcare requirements.</p></p>
