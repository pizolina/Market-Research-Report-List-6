<p><ul><li>Pixel-based Image Editors</li><li>Vector-based Image Editors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphic design software market is primarily divided into two main types: pixel-based image editors and vector-based image editors. Pixel-based image editors, such as Adobe Photoshop, manipulate images at the pixel level, making them ideal for photo editing and digital painting. In contrast, vector-based image editors, such as Adobe Illustrator, work with paths defined by mathematical equations, allowing for scalable graphics that maintain quality at any size, making them suitable for logos and illustrations. Both types serve distinct creative needs in design.</p></p>
