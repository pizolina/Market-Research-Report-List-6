<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sterility Test Isolator</li><li>Sterility Production Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical isolator market comprises two primary types: Sterility Test Isolators and Sterility Production Isolators. Sterility Test Isolators are designed for testing the sterility of pharmaceutical products, providing a controlled environment to prevent contamination during testing processes. In contrast, Sterility Production Isolators focus on maintaining aseptic conditions during the manufacturing of sterile products, ensuring that the entire production process remains free from microbial contamination. Both types are essential for compliance with stringent regulatory standards in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
