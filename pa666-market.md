Atactic polypropylene is amorphous, providing flexibility and softness, ideal for adhesives and sealants. Syndiotactic polypropylene features a regular alternation of molecular chains, granting excellent transparency and thermal resistance, making it attractive for consumer goods and industrial applications. Each type serves specific industry needs based on its unique properties.</p></p>
