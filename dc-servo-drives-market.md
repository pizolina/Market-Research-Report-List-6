<p><p>The DC servo drives market consists primarily of two types: brushed DC servo drives and brushless DC servo drives. Brushed DC servo drives utilize brushes and commutators to provide control, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, brushless DC servo drives eliminate brushes, providing higher efficiency, reduced maintenance, and enhanced performance. Brushless systems are increasingly favored for applications requiring precision and reliability, especially in robotics and automation, driving growth in the market.</p></p>
