<p><p>Hydrogen fuel tanks are categorized into five types based on their construction and materials. Type I tanks are made of metal and are heavy and rigid. Type II tanks combine metal with a composite wrap, offering moderate weight reduction. Type III tanks use a plastic liner with a composite shell for further weight savings. Type IV tanks feature a plastic liner and a full composite shell, making them the lightest option. Type V is an emerging concept, focusing on advanced materials and designs for enhanced efficiency.</p></p>
