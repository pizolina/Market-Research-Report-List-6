<p><p>The Narrowband Internet of Things (NB-IoT) chipset market is categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Stand-Alone**: These chipsets operate independently on dedicated spectrum, ensuring reliable connectivity with minimal interference.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Guard Band**: These chipsets utilize unused spectrum between two active bands, optimizing resource allocation while maintaining communication quality.</p><p>  </p><p>3. **In-Band**: These operate within existing cellular networks, sharing spectrum with LTE signals, which allows for more efficient integration and service deployment in urban areas.</p></p>
