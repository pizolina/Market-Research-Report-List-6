<p><p>Hot-melt based packaging adhesives are thermoplastic adhesives that are utilized in various packaging applications due to their strong bonding capabilities, quick setting time, and ease of use. These adhesives are typically applied in a molten state and solidify upon cooling, creating a durable bond without the need for solvents. Their versatility makes them suitable for a wide range of materials including cardboard, plastics, and metals, which are commonly used in food packaging, consumer goods, and industrial applications.</p><p>The hot-melt based packaging adhesives market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for sustainable and efficient packaging solutions.
