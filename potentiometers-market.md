<p><p>The potentiometers market is divided into two main types: linear and rotary potentiometers. Linear potentiometers feature a straight track where the wiper moves along the length, providing output that changes linearly with position. They're commonly used in applications like volume control and position sensing. Rotary potentiometers, on the other hand, consist of a rotating element where the wiper moves around a circular path, making them ideal for applications such as dial controls and angle measurements. Both types are essential in various electronic devices.</p></p>
