Positive Display Type panels feature dark characters on a light background, enhancing visibility in well-lit environments, ideal for applications requiring clarity. Conversely, Negative Display Type panels showcase light characters on a dark background, offering better readability in low-light conditions, making them suitable for environments where glare reduction is essential. These types cater to diverse consumer needs across various industries, ensuring optimal user experience.</p></p>
