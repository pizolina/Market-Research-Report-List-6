<p><p>The Surge Protection Diode market consists of two main types: ESD (Electro Static Discharge) and TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression). ESD diodes protect electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge, which can occur during handling or transportation. TVS diodes protect against transient voltage spikes, such as those from lightning strikes or power surges. Both types of diodes are crucial components in ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic devices in various industries.</p></p>
