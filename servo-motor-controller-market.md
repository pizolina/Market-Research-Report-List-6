<p><p>The servo motor controller market is categorized into 2-phase and 3-phase types. </p><p>2-phase servo controllers utilize two alternating current phases to control the motor's position and speed, offering simpler designs ideal for low torque applications. In contrast, 3-phase servo controllers employ three phases that provide smoother operation, higher efficiency, and greater torque, making them suitable for more demanding and industrial applications. Both types facilitate precise movement and control in various automation and robotics industries, catering to diverse performance needs.</p></p>
