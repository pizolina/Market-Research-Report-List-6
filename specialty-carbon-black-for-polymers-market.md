<p><p>Specialty carbon black for polymers includes various types that cater to specific applications. Lamp black is produced from the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, offering excellent hardness and color, ideal for coatings. Acetylene black, made from the thermal decomposition of acetylene, provides high conductivity, used in electrical applications. Gas black is derived from natural gas combustion, featuring low volatility and used in plastics. Other types may include channel black and furnace black, each tailored for distinct polymer performance and characteristics.</p></p>
