<p><p>The focused ultrasound system market is primarily divided into two types: ultrasound-guided focused ultrasound systems and MR-guided focused ultrasound systems. Ultrasound-guided systems utilize ultrasound imaging for real-time targeting and monitoring during procedures, offering quicker and more accessible treatment options. In contrast, MR-guided systems incorporate magnetic resonance imaging, providing enhanced precision and detailed visualization of tissues, making them suitable for complex treatments. Both types enable non-invasive therapies for various medical conditions, enhancing patient outcomes and expanding treatment possibilities.</p></p>
