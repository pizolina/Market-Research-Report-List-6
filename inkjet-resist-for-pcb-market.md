<p><p>Inkjet resists for PCB manufacturing are specialized materials that enable high-resolution printing of circuit designs. UV curable inkjet resists utilize ultraviolet light to cure the ink instantly, providing fast processing times and excellent resolution, ideal for complex patterns. In contrast, thermal curable inkjet resists require heat to initiate the curing process, offering better adhesion and durability. Both types serve the growing demand for precise and efficient PCB production, catering to industries like electronics and telecommunications.</p></p>
