<p><p>Multi-tenant SaaS markets are categorized into cloud-based and on-premises types. In contrast, on-premises solutions deploy the application within an organization's own infrastructure, providing greater control and customization but often requiring significant investment in hardware and software management. Each type caters to different business needs and preferences regarding security, performance, and resource allocation.</p></p>
