<p><p>The **Formaldehyde Scavengers Market** includes various types based on their mechanisms:  </p><p>1. **Neutralization Reaction**: Scavengers chemically react with formaldehyde, converting it into non-toxic compounds, effectively neutralizing its harmful effects.  </p><p>2. **Catalytic Decomposition**: These scavengers use catalysts to break down formaldehyde into simpler, harmless substances like water and carbon dioxide.  </p><p>3. **Oxidation-Reduction**: Scavengers oxidize formaldehyde into less harmful byproducts, reducing its concentration in the environment.  </p><p>4. **Sealing Plugging**: This method involves creating barriers or coatings to trap formaldehyde emissions, preventing its release into the air.  </p><p>Each type addresses formaldehyde mitigation through distinct chemical or physical processes.</p></p>
