<p><strong>The ABS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) sensor market can be categorized primarily into two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel speed, providing precise and reliable data for ABS control. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors function by generating an electrical signal in response to changes in magnetic fields produced by rotating components. Both sensor types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety and performance, making them essential components in modern automotive systems.</p></p>
