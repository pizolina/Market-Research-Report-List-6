<p><p>The industrial power tools market is characterized by various battery types that enhance performance and efficiency. Lithium-Ion batteries are popular for their lightweight design, long lifespan, and fast charging capabilities, making them ideal for high-demand applications. Nickel-Cadmium (Ni-Cad) batteries are known for their robustness and ability to deliver consistent power, although they are heavier and have a lower energy density. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries offer a balance between the two, providing better capacity than Ni-Cad while being more environmentally friendly. Each battery type caters to specific user needs and applications in the industrial sector.</p></p>
