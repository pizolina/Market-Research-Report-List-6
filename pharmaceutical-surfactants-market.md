<p><p>The pharmaceutical surfactants market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Anionic Surfactants** - Negatively charged, these enhance solubility and stability in formulations. </p><p>2. **Cationic Surfactants** - Positively charged, they offer antimicrobial properties and are often used in creams and ointments. </p><p>3. **Amphoteric Surfactants** - These contain both positive and negative charges, allowing versatility in formulations. </p><p>4. **Nonionic Surfactants** - With no charge, they are mild and used in a variety of applications. </p><p>Other surfactants include specialty and biosurfactants tailored for specific formulations.</p></p>
