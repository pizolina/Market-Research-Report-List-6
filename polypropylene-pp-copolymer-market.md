<p><strong>The Polypropylene (PP) Copolymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotactic Polypropylene</li><li>Atactic Polypropylene</li><li>Syndiotactic Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene copolymer market comprises three main types: isotactic, atactic, and syndiotactic polypropylene. Isotactic polypropylene features a highly ordered structure, offering high strength and rigidity, making it suitable for various applications like packaging and automotive components. Atactic polypropylene, lacking a crystalline structure, is flexible and rubber-like, often used in adhesives and sealants. Syndiotactic polypropylene, characterized by its alternating stereochemistry, showcases excellent thermal stability and is utilized in high-performance applications like textiles and packaging materials.</p></p>
