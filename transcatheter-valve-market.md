<p><p>The transcatheter valve market comprises three main types: Transcatheter Aortic Valve (TAV), Transcatheter Pulmonary Valve (TPV), and Transcatheter Mitral Valve (TMV). TAV primarily addresses aortic stenosis, offering less invasive solutions for valve replacement. TPV is utilized for pulmonary valve disorders, enabling repairs without open heart surgery. TMV is designed to treat mitral valve insufficiency or stenosis, facilitating minimally invasive procedures. Together, these technologies enhance patient outcomes by reducing recovery times and surgical risks associated with traditional heart surgeries.</p></p>
