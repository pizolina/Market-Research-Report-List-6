<p><p>Content Delivery Network (CDN) Software refers to a distributed network of servers that delivers web content, including images, videos, and applications, efficiently to users based on their geographical location. The demand for CDN solutions has surged due to the increasing online traffic, the proliferation of streaming services, and the rising importance of mobile internet access.</p><p>The Content Delivery Network (CDN) Software Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.9% during the forecast period. Key trends influencing this growth include the rise of edge computing, which allows data processing closer to the source, and the growing adoption of cloud-based CDN solutions. Additionally, enterprises are increasingly recognizing the need for enhanced security and improved content distribution strategies, driving investments in advanced CDN technologies. The integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning into CDN services for better performance analytics and optimization is also becoming common. Overall, the CDN market is poised for substantial growth as digital content consumption continues to escalate globally.</p></p>
