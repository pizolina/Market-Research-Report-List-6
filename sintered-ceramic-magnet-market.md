<p><ul><li>Dry Pressing</li><li>Wet Press Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sintered ceramic magnet market primarily comprises two main types: dry pressing and wet press molding. Dry pressing involves compacting ceramic powder into a desired shape under high pressure, producing magnets with high density and strength. In contrast, wet press molding combines the powder with a binding agent and water, allowing for more intricate shapes before drying and sintering. Each method offers distinct advantages, such as cost-efficiency, production speed, and the ability to achieve specific magnetic properties based on the application.</p></p>
