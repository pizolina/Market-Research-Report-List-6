<p><p>The wireless speaker market is primarily categorized into three technologies: Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and Infrared. Bluetooth technology offers convenience and portability, enabling quick pairing with devices for short-range audio streaming. Wi-Fi technology provides higher sound quality, longer range, and multi-room capabilities, allowing speakers to connect to home networks and stream music seamlessly. Infrared technology, although less common, relies on line-of-sight for audio transmission, suitable for specific environments. Each technology serves different consumer needs, shaping the dynamics of the wireless speaker market.</p></p>
