<p><strong>The Iron Core Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asynchronous</li><li>Synchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron core motors can be categorized into two main market types: asynchronous and synchronous motors. Asynchronous motors, often referred to as induction motors, operate without requiring synchronization with the supply current, making them highly efficient and widely used in industrial applications. Synchronous motors, on the other hand, maintain a constant speed in relation to the supply frequency, providing precise control and improved performance in specific applications. Both types serve distinct markets based on efficiency, performance, and application requirements.</p></p>
