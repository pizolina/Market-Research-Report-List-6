<p><p>Melt pressure transducers are critical for measuring pressure in high-temperature polymer processes. The market is primarily divided into two types: NaK-filled melt pressure transducers and oil-filled melt pressure transducers. NaK-filled transducers use a sodium-potassium alloy, allowing for greater sensitivity and stability at elevated temperatures, making them ideal for specific applications. Conversely, oil-filled transducers use hydraulic oil for pressure transmission, known for their durability and ease of calibration, suitable for a broader range of industrial environments.</p></p>
