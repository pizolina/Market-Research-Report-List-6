<p><strong>The Smart 3D Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Binocular Stereo Vision</li><li>Time of Flight</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart 3D Cameras market encompasses three main types: Binocular Stereo Vision, Time of Flight, and Structured Light. Binocular Stereo Vision uses two lenses to capture depth information by analyzing differences in images. Time of Flight cameras measure the time it takes for a light signal to return, providing precise distance measurements. Structured Light projects a known pattern onto the scene, assessing deformation to gauge depth. Each technology serves varying applications in robotics, automation, and augmented reality.</p></p>
