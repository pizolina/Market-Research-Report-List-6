<p><strong>The Mainline Rail Signalling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Train Control</li><li>Communication Based Train Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mainline Rail Signalling Systems market is divided into two primary types: Traditional Train Control and Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC). Traditional Train Control relies on fixed signals and trackside equipment for train movement and safety, ensuring separation between trains through physical distance and timing protocols. In contrast, CBTC uses real-time communication between trains and control centers, enhancing safety and efficiency by allowing closer train spacing and dynamic routing, ultimately supporting higher capacity and improved operational flexibility on rail networks.</p></p>
