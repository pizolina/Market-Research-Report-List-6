Chirped-pulse amplifiers enhance these pulse durations, providing significant energy boosts for high-intensity experiments. Tunable continuous wave lasers offer adjustable wavelengths, allowing precise applications in spectroscopy and medical uses. Together, these technologies drive advancements in research, industrial processing, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
