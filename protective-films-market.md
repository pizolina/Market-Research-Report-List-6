<p><p>The protective films market comprises two primary types: adhesive-coated and self-adhesive films. Adhesive-coated films are pre-applied with a layer of adhesive, allowing for easy application onto surfaces, providing durable protection against scratches, dust, and chemicals. In contrast, self-adhesive films have an inherent sticky backing, enabling straightforward placement without additional adhesives. Both types serve various industries, enhancing product longevity by safeguarding surfaces during production, transportation, or installation, thereby reducing the risk of damage and maintaining aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
