<p><strong>The Oral Contraceptive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combination</li><li>Progestin Only</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral contraceptive market is primarily categorized into three types: combination, progestin-only, and others. Combination pills contain both estrogen and progestin, effectively preventing ovulation and regulating menstrual cycles. Progestin-only pills, often preferred by those with estrogen sensitivities, work primarily by thickening cervical mucus to hinder sperm movement. The "others" category includes unique formulations like extended-cycle or emergency contraceptives, offering alternatives for specific needs. Together, these segments cater to diverse reproductive health requirements and preferences.</p></p>
