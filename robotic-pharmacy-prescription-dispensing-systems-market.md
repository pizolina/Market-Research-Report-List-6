<p><p>Robotic Pharmacy Prescription Dispensing Systems come in two main types: Centralized Dispensing Systems and Decentralized Dispensing Systems. Centralized Dispensing Systems involve a single pharmacy location where all prescriptions are filled and distributed. In contrast, Decentralized Dispensing Systems are set up in multiple locations, such as hospitals or clinics, allowing for quicker access to medications for patients. Both systems utilize technology and automation to accurately dispense medications, improving efficiency and patient safety in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
