<p><ul><li>Fixed Angle</li><li>Dynamic Angle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial inclination sensors are essential for measuring the tilt or angle of machinery and structures. The market is segmented into two main types: fixed angle and dynamic angle sensors. Fixed angle sensors provide a constant measurement at a predetermined angle, ideal for applications requiring stability. In contrast, dynamic angle sensors measure varying tilt angles in real-time, catering to applications with moving machinery or fluctuating conditions. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in industrial operations by ensuring accurate alignment and positioning.</p></p>
