<p><p>The research slide scanner market encompasses two primary types: brightfield and fluorescence. Brightfield imaging uses standard light to visualize stained samples, offering high-resolution images ideal for routine histopathology. In contrast, fluorescence imaging employs specific wavelengths of light to excite fluorescent markers, enabling the detection of specific proteins or cellular structures within samples. Each type serves distinct research needs, enhancing the capabilities of laboratories in fields such as cancer research, diagnostics, and molecular biology. Together, they advance understanding of complex biological systems.</p></p>
