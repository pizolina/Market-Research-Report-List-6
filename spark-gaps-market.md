<p><p>Spark gaps are voltage-controlled devices used for protection against electrical surges. The mini triggered spark gap is designed for low power applications and activates at specific voltage levels. Overvoltage spark gaps effectively manage excessive voltage by diverting electrical surges, ensuring system safety. Triggered vacuum spark gaps operate in a vacuum environment, offering enhanced performance in high-voltage applications, with rapid response times and reduced energy losses. Together, these spark gap types serve critical roles in various electrical protection systems.</p></p>
