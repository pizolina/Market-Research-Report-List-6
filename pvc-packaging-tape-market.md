<p><p>PVC packaging tape is widely used for sealing and securing packages. The market types include water-based ink and UV-curable variants. Water-based ink tapes use eco-friendly, solvent-free inks, making them safer for the environment and suitable for general-purpose applications. UV-curable tapes, on the other hand, utilize inks cured by ultraviolet light, offering faster drying times, enhanced durability, and resistance to abrasion, making them ideal for high-performance or industrial use. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs, balancing sustainability and functionality.</p></p>
