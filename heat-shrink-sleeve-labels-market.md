<p><p>Heat-shrink sleeve labels are categorized based on the material used, including PVC (polyvinyl chloride), PETG (polyethylene terephthalate glycol), OPS (oriented polystyrene), PE (polyethylene), PP (polypropylene), and COC (cyclic olefin copolymer) films. Each material offers distinct advantages such as flexibility, clarity, and resistance to various environmental factors. PVC is cost-effective and widely used, while PETG and COC provide excellent clarity and recyclability. OPS is lightweight and strong, whereas PE and PP are known for their barrier properties and versatility in packaging applications.</p></p>
