<p><p>The biohazard bags market is categorized into various types based on material, including LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), cellophane, and polypropylene. LDPE bags are flexible and resistant to stress, making them suitable for general biohazard waste. HDPE bags offer higher strength and tear resistance, ideal for sharp or heavy materials. Cellophane bags are biodegradable and offer visibility, often used in laboratories. Polypropylene bags provide excellent chemical resistance, ideal for hazardous material storage and transport, enhancing safety and compliance.</p></p>
