<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy-efficient powder coatings market is primarily categorized into thermoplastic and thermosetting types. Thermoplastic coatings can be re-melted and reshaped upon heating, offering excellent flexibility and durability, suitable for various applications. Conversely, thermosetting coatings undergo a chemical change when heated, resulting in a hard, inflexible finish that provides superior resistance to heat and chemicals. Both types contribute to energy efficiency by reducing waste and emissions during application, thus promoting environmentally sustainable practices in coating processes.</p></p>
