<p><p>The back grinding tapes market is segmented into UV type and non-UV type tapes, primarily used in the semiconductor industry for wafer thinning processes. UV type back grinding tapes are designed to be sensitive to ultraviolet light, allowing for easy removal after grinding. In contrast, non-UV type tapes do not require UV exposure for detachment, offering advantages in certain applications where UV exposure is impractical. Both types cater to different operational needs, influencing their market demand and applications.</p></p>
