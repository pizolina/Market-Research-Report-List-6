<p><p>The exhaust flange market comprises various types, including flared end butts and ball design flanges. Flared end butts feature a flared connection that allows for easy alignment and sealing, ensuring a secure exhaust system. On the other hand, ball design flanges utilize a spherical joint that enables rotational movement between connected parts, reducing stress and enhancing durability. Both designs focus on improving exhaust flow efficiency and minimizing leaks, catering to diverse automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
