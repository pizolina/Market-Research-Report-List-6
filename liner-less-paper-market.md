<p><p>Linerless paper markets consist of three main types: Direct Thermal, Thermal Transfer, and Laser. Direct Thermal uses heat-sensitive materials to produce images without ribbons, ideal for short-term labeling. Thermal Transfer employs a ribbon that transfers ink onto the substrate, providing durability and vibrant colors, suitable for long-lasting labels. Laser printing involves using toner and a heated fuser to create high-quality prints on various media, often used for detailed graphics and high-volume applications, ensuring versatility across industries.</p></p>
