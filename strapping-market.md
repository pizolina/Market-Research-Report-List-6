<p><p>Strapping markets encompass two primary types: steel strapping and plastic strapping, each serving distinct packaging needs. Steel strapping is robust and ideal for heavy-duty applications, providing high tensile strength for securing heavy loads, often used in industries like construction and steel manufacturing. Conversely, plastic strapping is lightweight, flexible, and corrosion-resistant, making it suitable for lighter loads and diverse applications, including retail and logistics. Both types are essential for ensuring the integrity and stability of goods during transport and storage.</p></p>
