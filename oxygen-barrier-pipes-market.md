<p><p>The oxygen barrier pipes market encompasses various types, including PE-RT, PEX, and PB oxygen barrier pipes. PE-RT (Polyethylene of Raised Temperature) pipes offer flexibility and resistance to high temperatures. PEX (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) pipes are known for their excellent durability and resistance to corrosion, making them suitable for diverse plumbing applications. PB (Polybutylene) pipes, while less common now, were once favored for their lightweight and ease of installation. Each type serves to prevent oxygen diffusion, which helps protect heating systems and prevents corrosion of connected metal components.</p></p>
