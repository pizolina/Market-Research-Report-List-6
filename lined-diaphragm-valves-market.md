<p><p>Lined diaphragm valves are specialized valves designed to control the flow of fluids, particularly in applications involving corrosive or abrasive substances. These valves feature a flexible diaphragm that separates the fluid from the actuating mechanism, ensuring a leak-tight seal. The lining, typically made from materials like PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), protects the valve body from aggressive chemicals while maintaining functionality.</p><p>The Lined Diaphragm Valves Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in industries such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage. The need for safety and reliability in fluid handling, especially in corrosive environments, is propelling market expansion.
