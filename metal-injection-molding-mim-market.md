<p><p>Metal Injection Molding (MIM) is an advanced manufacturing process that combines metal powder and a binding agent to create complex parts. The market includes several key methods: the Carbonyl Method uses iron carbonyl to produce fine metal powders; High-Pressure Gas Atomization involves injecting molten metal into high-pressure gas, forming fine droplets; and Ultra-High Pressure Water Atomization utilizes water jets to atomize molten metal, generating uniform particles. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of particle size, density, and application suitability.</p></p>
