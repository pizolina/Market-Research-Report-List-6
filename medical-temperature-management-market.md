<p><strong>The Medical Temperature Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Patient Cooling Systems</li><li>Patient Warming Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical temperature management market comprises two primary types: patient cooling systems and patient warming systems. Patient cooling systems are designed to reduce body temperature in cases of hyperthermia, heat stroke, or during surgery, enhancing patient recovery and outcomes. Conversely, patient warming systems aim to maintain or elevate body temperature to prevent hypothermia, improve surgical safety, and promote recovery in critically ill patients. Both systems are essential in diverse clinical settings, ensuring optimal thermal regulation for patient care.</p></p>
