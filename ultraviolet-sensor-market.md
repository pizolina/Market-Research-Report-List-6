<p><strong>The Ultraviolet Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UVA Sensor</li><li>UVB Sensor</li><li>UVC Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultraviolet sensor market encompasses three primary types: UVA sensors, UVB sensors, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors detect longer wavelengths (320-400 nm), primarily used for monitoring sunlight exposure and assessing UV-related skin damage. UVB sensors, operating in the 280-320 nm range, are essential for measuring sunburn risk and optimizing outdoor activities. UVC sensors, targeting shorter wavelengths (100-280 nm), are crucial for sterilization applications, as they effectively inactivate microorganisms. Each sensor serves distinct functions across various industries, including health, environmental monitoring, and safety.</p></p>
