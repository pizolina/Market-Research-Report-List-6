<p><strong>The Pallet Pooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tray static leasing</li><li>Tray dynamic flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pallet pooling is a system in which companies share and reuse pallets to reduce costs and increase efficiency in their supply chains. </p><p>In tray static leasing model, companies lease a set number of trays for a fixed period of time. This allows them to have a consistent supply of trays but may lead to underutilization.</p><p>On the other hand, tray dynamic flow market operates on a pay-per-use basis, allowing companies to only pay for the trays they actually use. This model promotes flexibility and helps in optimizing tray utilization.</p></p>
