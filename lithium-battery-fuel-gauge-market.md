<p><p>The Lithium Battery Fuel Gauge market is divided into two main types: Single-Cell and Multi-Cell gauges. Single-Cell gauges monitor the charge levels of individual lithium cells, providing accurate readings for smaller electronic devices like smartphones and wearables. In contrast, Multi-Cell gauges manage the performance of multiple cells within larger battery packs, commonly found in electric vehicles and renewable energy systems. Both types are crucial for enhancing battery efficiency, safety, and lifespan by ensuring optimal performance management.</p></p>
