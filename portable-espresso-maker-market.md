<p><p>The portable espresso maker market comprises three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual models require the user to exert pressure for extraction, offering complete control over the brewing process, making them ideal for coffee enthusiasts. Semi-automatic machines automate water flow but require manual coffee tamping, balancing convenience with skill. Automatic models streamline the brewing process further by controlling grind size, water flow, and temperature, appealing to those seeking consistency and ease of use, especially while traveling.</p></p>
