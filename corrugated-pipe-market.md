Double-wall corrugated pipes have an additional smooth inner layer, enhancing flow efficiency and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications like stormwater management and sewer systems. Both types are lightweight, cost-effective, and resistant to corrosion, catering to diverse industrial and infrastructure needs.</p></p>
