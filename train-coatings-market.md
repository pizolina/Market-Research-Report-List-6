<p><p>The train coatings market is primarily divided into two main types: primer and top coat. Primers serve as a foundational layer, enhancing adhesion, corrosion resistance, and overall durability of the surface. They prepare the substrate for additional coatings while preventing rust and degradation. Top coats, on the other hand, provide the final protective layer, enhancing aesthetic appeal and resistance to wear, UV exposure, and environmental factors. Together, they ensure the longevity and performance of train exteriors in varying conditions.</p></p>
