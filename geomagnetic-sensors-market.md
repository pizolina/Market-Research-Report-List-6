<p><p>Geomagnetic sensors are categorized into various types based on their packaging and mounting specifications. LGA (Land Grid Array) sensors feature a flat array of contacts and provide robust connectivity for compact applications. WLCSP (Wafer-Level Chip Scale Package) sensors, being smaller and thinner, are ideal for portable devices with limited space. Other variations may include traditional through-hole or surface-mount packages, catering to diverse industrial and consumer electronics needs. Each type offers unique benefits for accuracy, size, and ease of integration.</p></p>
