<p><ul><li>X-ray Based</li><li>Gamma-ray Based</li><li>Proton-beam Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiosurgery and radiotherapy robotics encompass various treatment modalities targeting tumors with precision. X-ray based systems deliver high-energy X-rays to destroy cancer cells, commonly used in conventional radiation therapy. Gamma-ray based systems utilize gamma radiation from isotopes, often employed in stereotactic radiosurgery for lesions. Proton-beam based systems use charged particles to precisely target tumors while minimizing damage to surrounding tissue. Collectively, these market segments provide advanced, targeted treatment options for oncology patients, enhancing therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
