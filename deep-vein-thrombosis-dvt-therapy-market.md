Drugs primarily include anticoagulants and thrombolytics that help dissolve clots and prevent new ones. Venous stents are devices used to maintain open blood vessels and improve blood flow, reducing the likelihood of recurrence. Compression therapy involves the use of specialized stockings or wraps to enhance venous circulation and minimize swelling, thus playing a crucial role in managing DVT symptoms and complications.</p></p>
