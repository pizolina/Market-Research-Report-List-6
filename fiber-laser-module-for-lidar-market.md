<p><ul><li>Long Pulsed</li><li>Short Pulsed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber laser modules for LiDAR applications are classified into long pulsed and short pulsed types, depending on their pulse duration. Long pulsed lasers emit energy over a longer duration, resulting in higher average power and better penetration into dense materials, making them suitable for long-range applications. In contrast, short pulsed lasers produce brief, intense bursts of light, which allow for high-resolution measurements and improved accuracy in detecting close-range objects. Both types play vital roles in enhancing LiDAR system performance.</p></p>
