<p><p>3D printing with metal powders includes two main market types: Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). DMLS involves using a high-powered laser to fuse metal powder together layer by layer, creating intricate and complex parts. SLM, on the other hand, involves melting and fusing metal powders together using a laser. Both methods are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare for rapid prototyping and producing high-quality metal parts.</p></p>
