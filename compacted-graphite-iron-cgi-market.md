<p><p>Compacted Graphite Iron (CGI) is a high-performance material that combines the benefits of ductile iron and gray iron, offering enhanced strength and thermal conductivity. In the CGI market, two primary forms are recognized: spheroids and nodules. Spheroids feature rounded graphite formations, providing improved ductility and impact resistance, while nodules are characterized by their elongated shapes, enhancing tensile strength and wear resistance. These variations cater to diverse applications, including automotive and heavy-duty machinery, where durability and performance are crucial.</p></p>
