<p><p>The LTE antenna market comprises two primary types: Normal Type and Compact Type. Normal Type antennas are larger and typically offer extended coverage and higher gain, making them suitable for urban areas with high user density. In contrast, Compact Type antennas are smaller and designed for ease of installation in limited spaces, often used in modern urban environments or on smaller devices.
