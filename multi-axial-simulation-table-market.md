<p><ul><li>Electric Simulation Table</li><li>Hydraulic Simulation Table</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-Axial Simulation Table market is categorized into Electric Simulation Tables and Hydraulic Simulation Tables. Electric Simulation Tables utilize electric actuators for precise control and are preferred for applications requiring high accuracy and flexibility in testing various loading conditions. In contrast, Hydraulic Simulation Tables use hydraulic systems to simulate multi-axial loading, offering greater force output suitable for heavy-duty testing scenarios. Both types are essential for assessing materials and structural responses under realistic conditions across diverse industries.</p></p>
