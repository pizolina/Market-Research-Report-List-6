<p><p>The AAA battery market comprises three main types: ordinary, alkaline, and rechargeable batteries. Alkaline batteries offer improved energy density and longevity, making them popular for everyday devices. Rechargeable batteries, like NiMH or Li-ion, provide eco-friendly alternatives, allowing multiple uses and reduced waste over time.
