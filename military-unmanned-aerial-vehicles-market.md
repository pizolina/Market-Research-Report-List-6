<p><p>The military unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV) market is categorized into two primary types: fixed wing and rotary wing. Fixed wing UAVs are designed for longer endurance and greater range, making them suitable for surveillance, reconnaissance, and strike missions. In contrast, rotary wing UAVs, or drones, excel in vertical takeoff and landing, offering agility and the ability to hover, making them ideal for close support, tactical reconnaissance, and urban operations. Both types play vital roles in modern military operations.</p></p>
