<p><p>The Aircraft Anti-icing and Rain Protection System market includes Thermal Anti-icing and Liquid Anti-icing systems. Thermal Anti-icing uses heat, often from engine bleed air or electric elements, to prevent ice formation on critical surfaces like wings and engine inlets. Liquid Anti-icing involves spraying deicing fluids, such as glycol-based solutions, to melt or prevent ice buildup before takeoff. Both systems ensure safe flight operations by maintaining aerodynamic efficiency and visibility, crucial in adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
