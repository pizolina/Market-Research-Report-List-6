<p><p>The OLED blue emitter market comprises various technologies, each offering unique properties. Fluorescent emitters provide basic efficiency but limited lifespan. TADF emitters enable enhanced efficiency through thermally activated delayed fluorescence, improving performance. Hyperfluorescence emitters combine fluorescent and phosphorescent properties for high efficiency and brightness. Phosphorescent emitters, especially in light blue tones for lighting applications, achieve long lifetimes and high color purity. Each type serves distinct applications, addressing energy efficiency, longevity, and color quality in OLED displays and lighting solutions.</p></p>
