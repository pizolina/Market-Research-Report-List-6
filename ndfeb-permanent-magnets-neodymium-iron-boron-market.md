<p><p>NdFeB permanent magnets, made from neodymium, iron, and boron, are known for their exceptional magnetic strength and resistance to demagnetization. The market is primarily divided into two types: sintered and bonded NdFeB magnets. Sintered NdFeB magnets are produced through powder metallurgy, offering high performance and durability, commonly used in industrial applications. Bonded NdFeB magnets, manufactured by combining magnetic powder with a polymer, provide flexibility in shapes and sizes, making them ideal for high-volume production in consumer electronics and automotive sectors.</p></p>
