<p><p>The orthodontic product market encompasses various types, including dental braces, molar bands, wires, retainers, and other devices. Dental braces are the primary treatment for misaligned teeth, while molar bands are used to anchor braces to the back teeth. Wires connect the brackets and apply pressure to shift teeth. Retainers are used post-treatment to maintain teeth positioning. Other products may include orthodontic adhesives, expanders, and aligners, all contributing to correcting dental alignment and improving oral health.</p></p>
