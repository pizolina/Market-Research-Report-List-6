<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Mineral Fluid Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine mineral fluid lubricants market comprises three main types: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are derived from saturated hydrocarbons, offering excellent thermal stability and low volatility, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Naphthenic lubricants, characterized by their cyclic structure, provide good solvency and are often used in specialized applications. Aromatic lubricants contain aromatic hydrocarbons, offering superior lubrication properties but may have environmental concerns. Each type serves distinct needs in engine performance and protection.</p></p>
