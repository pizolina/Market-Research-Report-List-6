<p><p>Desiccant dehumidification rotors are hygroscopic systems designed to remove moisture from the air using materials that absorb water vapor. This technology is increasingly utilized in various applications, including HVAC systems, industrial processes, and food storage, where maintaining low humidity levels is crucial for product quality and efficiency.</p><p>The Desiccant Dehumidification Rotors Market is experiencing notable growth, driven by rising awareness of energy efficiency and indoor air quality.
