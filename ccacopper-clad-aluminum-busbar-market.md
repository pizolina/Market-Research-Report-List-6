<p><p>Copper Clad Aluminum (CCA) busbars are conductive materials that combine the benefits of both copper and aluminum, featuring a layer of copper bonded to an aluminum core. CCA busbars are widely used in various industries, including power distribution, renewable energy, and telecommunications, due to their cost-effectiveness and efficiency.</p><p>The CCA busbar market is poised for expansion, driven by increasing demand for lightweight and efficient electrical components.
