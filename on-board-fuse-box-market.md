<p><p>The On-Board Fuse Box market is categorized into two primary types: Interior and Under-Hood. Interior fuse boxes are located inside the vehicle cabin, typically near the dashboard or steering column, providing easy access for drivers to manage electrical systems like lights, wipers, and infotainment.
