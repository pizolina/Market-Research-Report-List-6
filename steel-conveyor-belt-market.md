<p><p>The steel conveyor belt market includes two primary types: strand steel cord rubber conveyor belts and layer steel cord rubber conveyor belts. Strand steel cord belts utilize high-tensile strands embedded within rubber for enhanced strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Layer steel cord belts consist of multiple layers of steel cords, providing flexibility and support for various materials and conditions. Both types are crucial for efficient material handling in industries like mining, manufacturing, and logistics.</p></p>
