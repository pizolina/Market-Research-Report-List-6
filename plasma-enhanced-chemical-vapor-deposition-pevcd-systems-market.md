<p><p>Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PEVCD) systems are essential for thin-film deposition in various applications. Parallel Plate Type PECVD systems involve two parallel electrodes, providing uniform film deposition across large substrates, making them suitable for flat panel displays and solar cells. Tube Type PECVD systems utilize a tubular chamber, allowing for the processing of long and cylindrical substrates, ideal for optical fibers and small components. Both types cater to distinct market needs based on substrate size and application requirements.</p></p>
