<p><ul><li>Double-sided</li><li>Single-sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical nonwoven adhesive tapes market comprises two primary types: double-sided and single-sided tapes. Double-sided adhesive tapes feature adhesive on both sides, facilitating secure attachment of various materials or devices to skin or dressings, making them ideal for wound care and securing electrodes. Single-sided adhesive tapes, on the other hand, have adhesive on one side and are typically used for securing dressings, bandages, and other medical materials to the skin, promoting effective wound management and protection.</p></p>
