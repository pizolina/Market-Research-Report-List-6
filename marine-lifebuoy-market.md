<p><p>The marine lifebuoy market is primarily divided into two main types: Integral Lifebuoys and Horseshoe Lifebuoys. Conversely, Horseshoe Lifebuoys have a U-shaped design, often featuring a solid structure for easy handling and retrieval. Both types are essential safety equipment on vessels, ensuring compliance with maritime regulations and enhancing safety during emergencies at sea.</p></p>
