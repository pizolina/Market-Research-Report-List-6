<p><strong>The Continuous Wave Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unmodulated Continuous Wave</li><li>Modulated Continuous Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Wave Radar (CWR) technology is divided into two main market types: unmodulated continuous wave and modulated continuous wave. Unmodulated continuous wave radar transmits a constant frequency signal, primarily used for applications like speed detection. In contrast, modulated continuous wave radar employs frequency or phase modulation, allowing for enhanced target detection and range measurement. This versatility makes modulated continuous wave radar suitable for applications such as automotive, aviation, and marine sensing, where precision and reliability are critical.</p></p>
