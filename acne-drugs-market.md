<p><p>The acne drugs market is categorized into two main types: injection acne drugs and external acne drugs. In contrast, external acne drugs include topical treatments such as creams, gels, and lotions that deliver active ingredients like benzoyl peroxide, salicylic acid, or retinoids directly to the skin, aiming to unclog pores and reduce breakout frequency. Both types serve distinct yet complementary roles in acne management.</p></p>
