<p><ul><li>Resistive Touch Controllers</li><li>Capacitive Touch Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen controller market is divided into two primary types: resistive and capacitive touch controllers. Resistive touch controllers operate by detecting pressure on the screen, allowing for greater accuracy with styluses or gloves, making them suitable for industrial and medical applications. Capacitive touch controllers utilize the electrical properties of the human body, enabling multi-touch capabilities and offering a more intuitive user experience. They are commonly used in smartphones and tablets due to their responsiveness and clarity.</p></p>
