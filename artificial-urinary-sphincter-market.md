<p><p>The Artificial Urinary Sphincter (AUS) is a medical device designed to manage urinary incontinence, particularly in males post-prostatectomy or individuals with detrusor underactivity. It consists of a cuff placed around the urethra, a pressure-regulating balloon, and a control pump. When activated, it allows for voluntary urination while maintaining continence during periods of inactivity.</p><p>The Artificial Urinary Sphincter Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, driven by factors such as increasing prevalence of urinary incontinence, the growing aging population, and advancements in medical technology.
