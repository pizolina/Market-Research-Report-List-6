<p><ul><li>Inorganic Terrazzo</li><li>Epoxy Terrazzo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The terrazzo flooring market comprises primarily two types: inorganic terrazzo and epoxy terrazzo. Inorganic terrazzo is made using cement and natural stones, offering durability and a classic aesthetic suitable for high-traffic areas. It typically requires a lengthy curing process. In contrast, epoxy terrazzo utilizes resin-based materials, allowing for quicker installation and a wide range of colors and designs, making it ideal for modern spaces. Both types provide low maintenance and long-lasting finishes, catering to diverse architectural needs.</p></p>
