<p><p>The high strength aluminum alloys market is primarily segmented into two types: expandable alloys and casting alloys. Expandable alloys are designed for applications requiring lightweight yet robust materials, commonly used in aerospace and automotive industries. These alloys offer superior strength-to-weight ratios and are often forged or extruded. On the other hand, casting alloys are utilized in applications demanding complex shapes and high durability. They provide excellent fluidity during the casting process, making them suitable for intricate components in various industries.</p></p>
