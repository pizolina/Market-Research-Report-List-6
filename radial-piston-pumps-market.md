<p><ul><li>Single-stage Pump</li><li>Multi-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial piston pumps are classified into single-stage and multi-stage types based on their design and operational capabilities. Single-stage pumps consist of one cylinder and deliver a fixed flow rate, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. In contrast, multi-stage pumps incorporate multiple cylinders, allowing for increased pressure and flow flexibility, ideal for high-demand systems. Both types are utilized in various industries, including hydraulic systems and machinery, where efficient fluid transfer is essential for performance and reliability.</p></p>
