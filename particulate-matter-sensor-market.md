<p><p>The particulate matter sensor market is categorized into PM2.5, PM10, and other types. PM2.5 sensors detect particles with diameters less than 2.5 micrometers, which can penetrate deep into the lungs and pose serious health risks. PM10 sensors measure particles up to 10 micrometers, which include pollen and dust. The "Others" category encompasses sensors for larger particles or specific pollutants. Each type is crucial for air quality monitoring, environmental research, and regulatory compliance, addressing various health and environmental concerns.</p></p>
