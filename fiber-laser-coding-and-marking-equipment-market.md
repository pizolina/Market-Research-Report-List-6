<p><p>Fiber laser coding and marking equipment refers to technology used for permanent labeling on various materials. In mask marking, a predefined pattern is used to selectively expose parts of a surface, allowing for intricate designs. Array marking employs multiple lasers in a grid layout to enhance speed and efficiency, ideal for high-volume production. Scanning marking utilizes a movable laser beam to precisely etch patterns over larger areas, providing flexible and detailed marking solutions across diverse industries.</p></p>
