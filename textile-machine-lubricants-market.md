<p><p>The textile machine lubricants market is divided into two main types: mineral oil-based and synthetic oil-based lubricants. Mineral oil-based lubricants are derived from refining crude oil and offer good lubricating properties, cost-effectiveness, and are widely used in various textile applications. In contrast, synthetic oil-based lubricants are engineered for enhanced performance, providing superior thermal stability, resistance to oxidation, and better protection under extreme conditions, making them ideal for high-performance textile machinery.</p></p>
