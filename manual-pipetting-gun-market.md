<p><p>The manual pipetting gun market features primarily two types: single-channel and multi-channel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed for precise liquid transfer in individual volumes, making them ideal for tasks requiring accuracy and flexibility. In contrast, multi-channel pipettes allow simultaneous transfer from multiple channels, drastically increasing throughput for applications like high-throughput screening. Both types are widely utilized in laboratories for various applications, including research, clinical diagnostics, and quality control, emphasizing efficiency and precision in liquid handling tasks.</p></p>
