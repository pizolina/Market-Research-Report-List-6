<p><p>CMOS Image Sensors (CIS) for non-mobile applications are crucial in various sectors, including industrial, automotive, and security systems. The market is primarily divided into two types: Rolling Shutter and Global Shutter. Rolling Shutter captures images line by line, ideal for scenes with minimal motion, but can distort fast-moving objects. In contrast, Global Shutter captures the entire image simultaneously, preventing motion artifacts, making it suitable for high-speed applications like machine vision and automotive sensing, where precision is critical.</p></p>
