<p><p>The DNA probes-based diagnostics market comprises various types of probes, each serving specific purposes. Genomic probes target specific regions within an organism's genome, enabling the detection of genetic mutations or variations. cDNA probes are synthesized from complementary DNA and are essential in identifying gene expression patterns. Oligonucleotide probes, short sequences of nucleotides, are used for hybridization, allowing for precise detection of specific DNA or RNA sequences. Together, these probes enhance the accuracy and efficiency of genetic diagnostics and research.</p></p>
