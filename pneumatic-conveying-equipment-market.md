<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Conveying</li><li>Vacuum Pressure Conveying</li><li>Combined Conveying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic conveying equipment market features three primary types: positive pressure conveying, vacuum pressure conveying, and combined conveying. Positive pressure conveying uses air to push materials through a pipeline, ideal for transporting bulk solids over long distances. Vacuum pressure conveying, on the other hand, uses negative pressure to draw materials through the system, suitable for fragile items. Combined conveying merges both techniques, allowing for flexibility in material handling and efficient transport in various industrial applications.</p></p>
