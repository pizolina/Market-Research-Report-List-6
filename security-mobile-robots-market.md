<p><p>The security mobile robots market is categorized into two main types: screen integrated and non-screen integrated robots. Screen integrated robots feature interactive displays that provide real-time information and enable user interaction for monitoring and navigation. They can display alerts, stream video feeds, and facilitate communication. In contrast, non-screen integrated robots typically rely on audio signals, sensors, and pre-programmed responses for security tasks. These focus on surveillance and patrolling without interactive visual interfaces, often being more compact and cost-effective.</p></p>
