<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PU Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Water-borne</li><li>High Solids</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU coating market is categorized into four main types: solvent-borne, water-borne, high solids, and powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents, offering durability and excellent finish but raise environmental concerns. Water-borne coatings are eco-friendly, offering low VOC emissions, making them increasingly popular. High solids coatings contain a greater portion of solid content, providing thicker layers with reduced solvent use. Powder coatings are solvent-free, applied as a dry powder, and offer excellent durability and a variety of finishes.</p></p>
