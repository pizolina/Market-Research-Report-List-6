<p><p>The amorphous metal ribbons market is primarily categorized into iron-based and cobalt-based types. Iron-based amorphous metals are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness, excellent magnetic properties, and applicability in electrical transformers, inductors, and sensors. Cobalt-based amorphous metals, though more expensive, offer superior performance in high-temperature and high-frequency applications, making them suitable for advanced electronics and aerospace industries. Both types are characterized by their non-crystalline structure, which contributes to enhanced mechanical and magnetic properties compared to traditional crystalline metals.</p></p>
