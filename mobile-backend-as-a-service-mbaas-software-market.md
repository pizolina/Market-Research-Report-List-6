<p><p>Mobile Backend-as-a-Service (mBaaS) is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and web-based. In contrast, web-based mBaaS solutions operate through web applications, enabling developers to create and manage backend services through a browser interface. Both types facilitate rapid app development, reduce infrastructure management, and enhance scalability while simplifying integration with various mobile platforms.</p></p>
