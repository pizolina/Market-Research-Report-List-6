<p><strong>The Liquid-cooled Heat Sink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conductable</li><li>Immersive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid-cooled heat sink market can be categorized into two main types: conductable and immersive. Conductable heat sinks utilize liquid to transfer heat away from components through direct contact with surfaces, enhancing thermal efficiency. Immersive solutions, on the other hand, submerge electronic components in a cooling liquid, allowing for efficient heat dissipation across the entire assembly. Both types are crucial for managing heat in high-performance applications, such as data centers and advanced computing environments, where thermal management is vital for reliability and performance.</p></p>
